Blood Group Detection

Abstract


Abstract: Innovative method for blood group identification integrates ORB, SIFT algorithms for feature extraction, and CNN for classification, ensuring precise blood type detection with remarkable classification accuracy and resilience to image quality variations. Through extensive trials on diverse blood group imaging datasets, the effectiveness of your approach has been demonstrated, offering a potential solution to simplify blood group identification in medical settings and enhance transfusion management and patient care. By optimizing contrast and reducing noise in blood group photos before feature extraction, your approach enhances the quality of input data for the CNN model, leading to reliable predictions based on unique blood type patterns. The fusion of deep learning with feature extraction techniques not only boosts prediction accuracy but also holds promise for automating blood sample analysis, thereby advancing medical diagnostics and treatment procedures. Overall, your work shows great potential in revolutionizing blood type identification through a combination of advanced technologies in image processing and deep learning.

Note: To ensure reliable blood group detection findings, please utilize the spectrometer tool to create a near-infrared image.
